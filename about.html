    • OUR STORY

    Founded in 2022 in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, OGT ENGINEERING INSTALLATIONS established itself as an electrical, mechanical, and plumbing engineering service company engaging in electrical product installations, commissioning preventive maintenance, and automation. Our primary goal is to deliver quality results that exceed the expectations of our customers.
    With a focus on excellence and innovation, we specialize in providing cutting-edge solutions for the entire electro-mechanical HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning), plumbing, fire fighting, fire alarm, electrical (MV, LV, and LV), security and surveillance, as well as BMS, IT, and audio/visual systems. CCTV installation solutions and commissioning services are also offered. Our dedicated team of experts brings years of experience and a commitment to delivering top-tier electrical solutions tailored to our clients' specific needs. We are your go-to partner for reliable, efficient, and sustainable services.

    our vision is simple yet profound.

    At OGT ENGINEERING, our vision is simple yet profound: to be the leading force in electrical, mechanical and plumping engineering installations. We strive to electrify the world with innovation, quality, and precision. Our commitment to excellence drives us to deliver state-of-the-art solutions that empower our clients and inspire a sustainable and connected future. We envision a world where MEP systems are not just efficient but transformative, and we are dedicated to making that vision a reality.
