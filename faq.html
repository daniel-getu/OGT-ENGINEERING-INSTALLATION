    What services does OGT ENGINEERING offer?

    OGT ENGINEERING specializes in electrical, mechanical, and plumbing engineering installations. Their services include electrical installation, testing and commissioning, preventive maintenance, power system analysis, automation, building service systems design, and more.

    OGT ENGINEERING is based in Bole, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

    OGT ENGINEERING aims to be a leading force in electrical, mechanical, and plumbing engineering installations. They strive to electrify the world with innovation, quality, and precision while envisioning a sustainable and connected future.
